    About Us

    Skyline General Trading Limited is a Private Limited Company incorporated on 22nd May 2015.The company's main activities are industrial minerals mining, supply of Building and Construction materials and civil construction works.

    Our Mission

    The company's vision is to be the one one industrial minerals mining and supply company with the best quality material produce in the safest way.

    Our Vision

    The company's mission is to transform natural industrial minerals into propsperity for sustainable development.

    Location

    The company administrative offices are located along Mombasa road in Nairobi County. The company has also secured land for mining in Garissa county. Audit has been undertaken on the lands and sites and excavation & extraction of industrial minerals approved by National Environmental Management Authority (NEMA).

    business and financial profile

    This business and finacial profile sets out a summary of the company's activites, business volume and clientele, technical details pertinent to performance of the activities of the company and summary of the company's financial Position since inception.

    ACTIVITIES / PRODUCTS

    The company operates mines in Garissa. We supply Gypsum to National Cement Company Limited in Uganda, Burundi Cement Company Limited in Burundi, Rai Cement Limited in Kenya, among others. We supply Coal to Erdemann Property Limited in Mwingi, Kenya.

    MARKET

    In the last seven (7) years the construction industry has experienced tremendous growth with both the Private sector and the Government taking up large construction projects. Demand for building and construction materials especially cement has proportionately been on the rise.
    Being one of the main ingredients of cement, demand for Pozzolana and Gypsum is also on the rise. Cement manufacturing companies are willing to purchase large quantities of good quality Pozzolana and Gypsum.
    It is in this regard that the company has decided to invest in increasing its capacity to improve its core activities of excavation and supply of Gypsum. This it is doing through increasing its machinery, equipment and trucks and identifying new customers for its products.

    business prospects

    CURRENT POSITION

    The company is already supplying Gypsum to National Cement Company Limited in Uganda and Rai Cement Ltd in Kenya. It is also supplying coal to Erdemann Properties Limited in Kenya, among other clients. The company is also prospecting for clients in Rwanda and Congo among other international destinations.

    PROSPECTIVE POSITION

    With the growing construction and building industry in the region, the demand for cement and other construction materials are on the rise. Existing cement manufacturers are increasing their capacity and new investors setting up factories in the region. Skyline General Trading Limited is aware of this and is strategically placing itself to be a leading supplier of Gypsum, Pozzolana, Limestone, Bauxite and Coal in Kenya and the region. The company has invested heavily in both machinery, equipment, trucks and other resources i.e. Human Capital, Financial partners among others.

    OFFICE PREMISES

    The company's administrative activities are done in Nairobi, where the Managing Director, Heads of Department, and Accountants are located. The offices are well equipped and manned by qualified and competent staff.

    MACHINERY AND EQUIPMENT

    The company has massively invested in machinery and equipment requisite to run the business. The equipment have the capacity to increase business volumes and therefore the company is able to take on more contracts.

    SWOT Analysis

    strengths

    The company has invested in its own machinery .The company has all the necessary licenses to mine and transport. The company has a good financial base and thus capacity to take on large contracts of mining and transportation. The management is experienced and focused in growing the company to be a leader in the industry. The company is already involved in mining and transportation.

    weakness

    Reduced margins due to breakdowns of equipment.

    opportunities

    Demand for gypsum and Pozzolana, ingredients for the manufacture of cement is high. New cement companies have started operations in the country and therefore demand for Gypsum and Pozzolana is increasing. There are opportunities to supply Gypsum to Uganda, Congo and Rwanda where the reserves for Gypsum are not as adequate as in Kenya.

    Threats

    Inflationary pressure on the cost of doing business. Adverse weather conditions that might hamper mining. Environmental impact of the business. This is mitigated by proper environmental management, the company is certified to mine by NEMA.

    Environment, safety and community service

    The company has an elaborate policy on safety and health. The company has done an environmental impact assessment self audit which has been submitted and accepted by the National Environmental Management Authority (NEMA).
    Skyline General Trading Limited has also developed a Human Resource Manual to address all employee issues and also ensure that all matters relating to policy are addressed.
    The company is involved in Corporate Social Responsibility (CSR) activities (Activities that help our surrounding communities.)
    These include:
    • We offer employment to locals in areas where we operate.
    • Fetching fresh waters for villagers.
    • Ambulance services for the sick from the village 24 Hours every day incase of emergencies.
    • Routine road maintenance in areas where we have sites.
